Are You an Athlete
and a Smoker?

One cigarette a day can destroy 100 days of fitness. Today is the day to take the important steps toward tobacco-free living. Claim back your life and shrug off this bad habit once and for all. Please join our free smoking cessation workshop through your local HMO.











About this project:
Overview:
Smoking cigarettes among athletes is increasing every year, especially in team sports.
Effective anti-smoking campaigns need to evoke strong emotions, you can't just preach that smoking is unhealthy.
When it comes to health and design, I ask myself one question: What is the emotional territory that I'm trying to cover?
The task:
As part of the annual advertising campaign, I was asked by the Ministry of Health to design a series of web and landing pages that will attract the attention of athletes who smoke cigarettes.
My role:
I led UX/UI and visual design. Designed digital assets, collaborated with advertising director, developers and ministry officials.
