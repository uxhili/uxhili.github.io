Are You an Athlete
and a Smoker?

Smoking cigarettes can be an easy habit to pick up and a hard one to quit. Today is the day to take those important steps toward tobacco-free living. Claim back your life and shrug off this bad habit once and for all. Please join our special smoking cessation workshop through your HMO.










About this project:
Overview:
Smoking cigarettes among athletes is increasing every year, especially in team sports.
The task:
I was asked by the Ministry of Health to design a landing page that will attract the attention of athletes who smoke cigarettes.
My role:
Visual Design, UX/UI.
