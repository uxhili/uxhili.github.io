Overview

A video platform that uses an AI-driven content analysis engine to analyze and categorize video content in real-time
and powers a holistic distribution strategy in live stream videos.
The platform harnesses the capabilities of artificial intelligence to meticulously dissect video content frame by frame, rendering it searchable, quantifiable, personalized, and interactive.
