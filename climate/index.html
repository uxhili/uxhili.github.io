Climate Change Program












The Project:
Overview:
Act Now is the United Nations campaign for individual action on climate change.
One of the essential principles of teaching climate change to students is the message that global warming has consequences for the earth and human lives.
The digital resources help teachers guide students to take an active role and make responsible decisions when it comes to the environment.
First, the teacher schedule a visit to the climate change exhibition, and then the 4 weeks climate change program begins.
The task:
Create a platform for teachers where they can easily follow the 4 weeks climate change program that empower individual action to fight climate change.
