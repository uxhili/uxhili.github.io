About This Project:
Overview:
Tracking what you eat is one of the most important parts of diabetes management. Young adults often struggle to self-manage their diabetes during the transition from childhood to adulthood. This transition is noticeable in the ages 18-25, when students leave their home for college. Diabetes management may be complicated by reliance on fast food, busy schedules, late-night studying, lack of sleep and alcohol use. Planning meals in advance is the best way to ensure a balanced and nutritious diet.
The task:
Design a digital platform that continually motivates patients to manage diabetes effectively through dietary and meal planning. The design must be young, playful and inviting.
My role:
Visual Design, UX/UI, Interaction Design.
