About This Project:
Overview:
Sunday morning in New York always starts with a bagel and a coffee.
When covid-19 landed in the city, many elderly New Yorkers skipped this tradition and stayed at home.
The task:
I was asked by a local bagel shop to design a simple flow bagel ordering app that is easy enough for non-tech savvy individuals.
My role:
Visual Design, UX/UI, Interaction Design.
